# The Engagement Model — index

**Audience:** anyone running or selling a Meaningfy consulting engagement.

**Purpose:** the human canon for the **four-phase engagement model (P0–P3)** that sits *above* the
two-tier build methodology. This doc narrates and points; it never restates a skill's rules. The
phase *intentions* are owned by the
[`semantic-consulting-coach`](../../skills/consulting/semantic-consulting-coach/references/engagement-model.md)
engagement model — read that for the canonical definitions; read here for the map and the links.

> **Phases ≠ Modes.** "Phases P0–P3" are the commercial engagement model. The coach's
> Exploration / Alignment / Synthesis are *conversation modes*. They are orthogonal — do not conflate
> them (see the coach's
> [`engagement-model.md`](../../skills/consulting/semantic-consulting-coach/references/engagement-model.md)).

## The four phases at a glance

| Phase | Question it answers | Commercial | Owning skill(s) |
|-------|---------------------|------------|-----------------|
| **P0 Orientation** | "Is this even relevant to us?" | Free, bounded | [`semantic-consulting-coach`](../../skills/consulting/semantic-consulting-coach/SKILL.md) |
| **P1 Decision** | "What do we do first, and why?" | **Paid**, fixed frame | [`decision-package`](../../skills/consulting/decision-package/SKILL.md), [`proposal-writing`](../../skills/consulting/proposal-writing/SKILL.md), [`estimation`](../../skills/consulting/estimation/SKILL.md) |
| **P2 Execution** | "How do we build this well?" | Paid, scoped | the build tier → [`../ai-coding/two-tier-methodology.md`](../ai-coding/two-tier-methodology.md) |
| **P3 Partnership** | "How do we keep this alive?" | Paid, optional | *to be shaped* (governance / ops) |

The four phases are detailed in [`phases.md`](phases.md).

## Where this sits

P0–P1 are the **consulting tier** (selling decision-readiness). P2 is the **build tier** — the
two-tier methodology in [`../ai-coding/two-tier-methodology.md`](../ai-coding/two-tier-methodology.md)
takes over once a decision is contracted. P3 is post-decision continuity. The hand-off from P1 to P2
is the Decision Package's *ready-to-contract execution brief* (see
[`decision-package`](../../skills/consulting/decision-package/SKILL.md)).

## The commercial layer — TODO (to be shaped)

The **commercial mechanics** of the front-of-funnel are **not yet developed** — they are still to be
brainstormed and crystallised, and must not be invented here. Open items:

- **Qualification** — the criteria and signals that move a prospect from P0 to a paid P1.
- **Pre-sale** — nurture, discovery cadence, when to propose.
- **Sale** — closing mechanics, the proposal/SoW motion (skill exists:
  [`proposal-writing`](../../skills/consulting/proposal-writing/SKILL.md); the *commercial process*
  around it does not).
- **Marketing** — positioning, content, demand generation.
- **CRM & lead communication** — pipeline, follow-up, lead nurture.
- **Service packaging** — how P0–P3 bundle into named, sellable offers.
- **Fit-for-market** — segment fit, B2B vs B2G motion differences.

These are placeholders, not commitments. They will be shaped after the dogfood engagement, alongside
the coaching the [`semantic-consulting-coach`](../../skills/consulting/semantic-consulting-coach/SKILL.md)
provides on the business and service layers.
